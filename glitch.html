What is GLITCH?

We are an ecosystem for all gamers.

Majority of people video game in some form or the other, and we aim to capture that market and
explore unlimited opportunities to organize related events. We promote intercollege events as well,
inclusive to everybody, and pros are more than welcome to participate in our tournaments.
According to popular consensus, GLITCH was very much needed to create and support a healthy gaming community.
Meet like-minded gamers via our dedicated Discord Server and be a part of the community,with GLITCH.

Why a Separate Gaming Subchapter?

Previously BVPCSI has organized many gaming events. Our LAN Gaming events during the Annual
College Fest BVEST are by far the most popular, with students from across the city vying to win.
This immense enthusiasm for participation, and lack of an infrastructure for large organized games , has
compelled us towards the formation of GLITCH.
Gaming is something that people love, and it is healthy for them as well. It improves cognitive ability,
communication skills, coordination, team spirit, memory, and many other fancy words, but you just
want to play, right? Read on to know our ideas for the future, to make GLITCH events the most fun ever!
